Once you really get used to Wangfu, you will understand why it is an attractive school. Although some have little experience, but all can communicate with you like friends and help you. The teachers will not force you to learn, but the TOEFL SAT test date and the application deadline are always reminding you that you have to take the test to get results. It's your own business if you don't learn in the end anyway. In addition, there are elite classes in each grade, and there are big cows in the elite classes, so when you are in an environment with such people, people with ambitious will work harder. In fact, after going abroad, I found that the environment of Wangfu is really close to that of foreign countries, so you can let yourself go first and then see if you can find yourself. And the resources of Wangfu are really good. Let's talk about my personal experience first. First of all, we have our own TOEFL test center, and the school also moves to provide you with TPO and PSAT exams, so if you are preparing to go abroad, it is still a much better atmosphere than the general high school. Then there's AP, which is related to the quality of teaching I mentioned earlier. The teachers are not bad, but they don't push you or fill you in, so it depends on the individual. So let's just say that my sophomore year of high school scored 3 out of 5, but many of my classmates who took the same class as me certainly did not have this result, some 7 out of 5, some just abandoned the test. Survival of the fittest is also a form of elite selection, as is going to a foreign university.
